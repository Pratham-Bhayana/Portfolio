GSAP - Scroll Trigger
GSAP ScrollTrigger is a powerful plugin from the GreenSock Animation Platform (GSAP) that enables developers to create advanced scroll-based animations and interactions. It allows you to trigger animations based on the user’s scroll position, control animations across different scroll points, and synchronize animations with the scroll timeline for a seamless, engaging user experience. With ScrollTrigger, you can effortlessly add dynamic effects and transitions that enhance the visual appeal and functionality of your website.
